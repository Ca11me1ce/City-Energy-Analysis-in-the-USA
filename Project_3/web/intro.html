Introduction

In order to have a better understanding of energy consumption and promote the development of energy conservation, this project consists of 8 components to complete.

  1. Obtain a detailed view of how energy consumption occurs in all fields of our daily life
  2. Analyze and review the current situation of energy consumption in the United States in recent years
  3. Draw the conclusion of the factors that affect energy consumption and the relationship among the factors
  4. Classify the cities based on the type of energy usage and the levels of energy usage: The types of energy use are industrial, commercial and residential. The levels of energy usage contain three levels-very high, high and normal. Set these levels on cities to reflect their energy usage.
  5. Analyze the energy usage for each state by putting together the levels mentioned before for all the cities in the same state.
  6. Speculate the state current situation.
  7. Use the historical data training model to predict the future energy use of each city and each state.
  8. Give the state government some suggestions on energy use and future development through the current status of the state and the prediction result of the future.

Finally, this project assigns corresponding levels to all cities and get the energy consumption levels of all states by aggregating the data of cities. Also lists factors which have huge impacts on energy consumptions and the influence of greenhouse emission.

Background

With the development of human society, energy consumption and carbon dioxide emissions are increasing day by day, bringing great climate and environmental pressure, and threatening the survival of human society. As an area with intensive human activities, cities are the primary sources of energy consumption and carbon emissions, and their governance and development in energy conservation and emission reduction are increasingly valued by all countries. Energy consumption, monitoring, and management are crucial components of sustainable, eco-friendly infrastructures now and into the future.

In the past, a building energy consumption analysis system is available which focuses on accurately determining building energy expenditures, performance, and costs. Besides, there is also energy consumption and cost-benefit analysis of hybrid and electric city buses. In 2017, real-time predictive systems for intelligent energy monitoring and management of electrical power networks are available. However, these systems are good tools for administrators to monitor and manage the electricity system to avoid any failure and interact with users. Instead of focusing on specific energy consumption, such as bus fuel, air-conditioning electricity consumption or management tools for owners, a need exists to analyze energy consumption on a larger scale, including most fields of our daily life and show the detailed analysis results or predicted energy consumption to the public. The object of this project is to provide intuitive senses about energy consumptions.
